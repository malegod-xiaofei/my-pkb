### 仇恨言论检测项目的数据
+ 本项目中用到的数据集来源于Facebook平台开放出来的多模态检测任务数据.
    - 原始图片: 存放于images文件夹中, 格式如 01235.png
    - 训练数据文件: train.jsonl
    - 测试数据文件: test.jsonl
    - 验证数据文件: dev.jsonl

---

+ 训练数据集展示如下, 文件中共有8500条样本数据.
    - 数据文件路径: /home/multimodal/data/train.jsonl

```plain
{"id":42953,"img":"img\/42953.png","label":0,"text":"its their character not their color that matters"}
{"id":23058,"img":"img\/23058.png","label":0,"text":"don't be afraid to love again everyone is not like your ex"}
{"id":15872,"img":"img\/15872.png","label":1,"text":"when you come home from a long day of suicide bombinb and your wife gives you this look"}
{"id":74386,"img":"img\/74386.png","label":1,"text":"we said we would never forget why are you voting them into our government?"}
```

---

+ 测试数据集展示如下, 文件中共有500条样本数据.
